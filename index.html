Get Started

WhatsApp Wrapped gives you a summary of your chat with another person!

To try it, simply open WhatsApp on your phone and click on one of your contacts.
Then click on Export Chat > Without Media

arrow_forward

Then simply upload the .txt file to this page and click submit!
